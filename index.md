# Privacy Policy — KVG Finance Tracker

**Last updated:** July 15, 2026

## Summary

KVG Finance Tracker is built to keep your financial data on your device. This policy explains exactly what that means and the specific cases where data leaves your device.

## What data the app collects

KVG Finance Tracker stores the following locally on your device, in a private database only the app itself can access:
- Names and phone numbers of parties you record (people/entities you lend to or borrow from)
- Loan, transaction, income, and expense records you enter
- A PIN you set to lock the app (stored as a one-way hash in your device's secure keystore — Keychain on iOS, Keystore-backed encrypted storage on Android — never as plain text, and never leaves your device)

**None of this is sent to us or to any third party automatically.** We do not operate a server that receives your financial data.

## When data does leave your device

Only when you take a specific, deliberate action:

1. **Local backup export** — when you tap "Export Backup," the app creates a file containing your data and hands it to your device's native share sheet. Where it goes after that (email, Google Drive, etc.) is entirely your choice.
2. **Cloud sync (not currently active)** — the app has code to support optional cloud backup via a Google account or email/password, but this feature is not enabled in the current version and is not accessible from the app's menus. If this changes in a future update, this policy will be updated accordingly and users will be able to opt in explicitly before any data leaves their device this way.
3. **Notifications** — reminder notifications about upcoming payments are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device using your operating system's notification system. No data is sent externally for this.

## Account information

Account sign-in (Google or email/password) exists in the app's code for a future cloud sync feature but is not currently enabled or reachable from any menu in this version. No account information is collected at this time.

## Advertising

The app displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use device advertising identifiers to serve ads, including personalized ads where permitted. See Google's Privacy Policy for how Google handles this data: https://policies.google.com/privacy — and Google's guide to controlling ad personalization: https://support.google.com/ads/answer/2662922

## Data Deletion — KVG Finance Tracker

**How to delete your data:**

1. **All data you've entered in KVG Finance Tracker** — your parties, loans, transactions, income sources, profile details, and PIN — is stored only on your own device. **Uninstalling the app permanently and immediately deletes all of it.** There is no server-side copy for us to separately delete, because none was ever created.
2. If you'd rather keep the app installed but clear your data first, use **Settings → Backup & Restore** — export a backup if you want a copy first, then clear app storage from your device's own Android system settings (Settings → Apps → KVG Finance Tracker → Storage → Clear Data) to reset the app without uninstalling it.
3. **Advertising identifier (the only data shared externally):** this app uses Google AdMob to display ads, which shares your device's advertising ID with Google. To delete or reset this identifier at any time, go to your device's **Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID** (wording varies slightly by Android version). This is controlled by Google, independent of this app, and takes effect immediately.

**What is retained, and for how long:** Nothing. KVG Finance Tracker does not operate a server that stores your financial data, so there is no retention period to disclose for it — the moment you uninstall or clear the app's storage, it's gone. Your advertising identifier is retained according to Google's own policies until you reset it via the device setting above; we do not separately store or retain a copy of it.

**Contact:** For any question about this that isn't answered above, contact kvgfinanceapp@gmail.com.
